Ek Khoobsurat Sher

Ek khoobsurat sher likha hai Kagaz pe tera naam likha hai

Tere aandhi mein khokar hi
Leheron ko kinara dikha hai

Banjar jameen thi barso se
Ab jaake uspe phool khila hai

Zindagi toh kab se hi thi
Par use jeevan ab mila hai

Kuch nazm kuch sher likha hai
Har sher mein tera naam likha hai
